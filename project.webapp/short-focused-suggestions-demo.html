Short & Focused Content Suggestions

What's Improved

✅ Shorter Suggestions

Suggestions are now 1-2 sentences max, not long paragraphs

✅ Focused Changes

Only specific sentences are replaced, not entire sections

✅ Accurate Replacements

Shows exact text to replace with precise improvements

✅ Simple & Effective

Minimal but impactful changes that improve content quality

Example 1: Short Sentence Replacement

Replace this text:

"Our product is good."

with
Replace with this text:

"Our productivity platform increases team efficiency by 40%."

Note: Short, specific improvement with concrete benefit.

Example 2: Focused Paragraph Improvement

Replace this text:

"We have been in business for many years."

with
Replace with this text:

"Since 2015, we have served over 500 enterprise clients across 15 industries."

Note: Specific details with concrete numbers and timeframe.

Example 3: Long Paragraph Break (Only for Very Long Paragraphs)

Replace this text:

"Our company offers various services to customers. We have been in business for many years and have a good reputation. Our team is experienced and knowledgeable. We provide excellent customer support and have won several awards. Our products are high quality and competitively priced. We serve clients worldwide and have offices in multiple countries."

with
Replace with this text:

"Our company offers various services to customers. We have been in business for many years and have a good reputation."

"Our team is experienced and knowledgeable. We provide excellent customer support and have won several awards."

Note: Only breaks very long paragraphs (500+ chars) into logical chunks.
